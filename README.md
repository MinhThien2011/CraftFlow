# 🎨 CraftFlow
### Smart Inventory Management and Craft Production Coordination System

CraftFlow is a web-based platform designed to help craft workshops efficiently manage materials, inventory, production processes, and product pricing.  
The system centralizes operational data and provides AI-assisted tools to improve productivity and decision-making.

---

## 🚀 Overview

Many small craft businesses manage their operations manually using spreadsheets or paper records. This often leads to:

- Inventory miscalculations
- Production inefficiencies
- Difficulty tracking costs
- Inconsistent product pricing

CraftFlow solves these problems by providing a centralized system that manages materials, tracks production, and integrates AI services to assist with pricing and product descriptions.

---

## ✨ Key Features

### 🔐 User & Access Management
- Secure authentication system
- Role-based access control (Admin / Staff)

### 🧱 Material Management
- Create and update materials
- Define material units and costs
- Track material usage

### 📦 Inventory Management
- Stock in / stock out tracking
- Automatic material deduction during production
- Inventory threshold alerts
- Inventory history tracking

### 🏭 Production Management
- Create and manage products
- Define Bill of Materials (BOM)
- Create production orders
- Track production status

### 🤖 AI Integration
- AI price recommendation for products
- AI-generated product descriptions

### 📊 Reporting & Analytics
- Production cost analysis
- Profit estimation
- Inventory reports
- Financial reports
